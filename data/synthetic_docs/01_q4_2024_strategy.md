# Q4 2024 Strategic Plan
## Executive Summary

**Document Type:** Strategic Planning
**Department:** Executive Office

**Author:** Jane Smith, Chief Strategy Officer
**Classification:** Internal Use Only

This document outlines Unify Consulting's strategic priorities for the fourth quarter of 2024, focusing on market expansion, service innovation, and operational excellence.

---

## Strategic Priorities

### 1. Market Expansion

Our Q4 2024 market expansion strategy focuses on three key verticals:

**Healthcare Sector**
- Target 15 new enterprise clients in healthcare systems
- Leverage our AI Systems & Engineering capabilities to address HIPAA-compliant data analytics needs
- Expected revenue impact: $2.4M in Q4, $8.5M annual recurring

**Financial Services**
- Expand presence in regional banks and credit unions
- Focus on AI adoption consulting and compliance automation
- Partner with 3 major fintech platforms for referral agreements

**Manufacturing**
- Address supply chain optimization through AI-powered demand forecasting
- Target mid-market manufacturers ($50M-$500M annual revenue)
- Pilot program with 5 key prospects in automotive supply chain

### 2. Service Innovation

**AI Adoption Accelerator Program**
- Launch comprehensive 8-week program for clients new to AI
- Includes assessment, roadmap development, pilot implementation
- Pricing: $75K base + $25K per additional pilot
- Goal: Onboard 12 clients in Q4

**Data Engineering as a Service**
- New recurring revenue model for ongoing data pipeline management
- Tiered pricing: Starter ($5K/mo), Professional ($15K/mo), Enterprise (custom)
- Focus on companies with 10+ data sources needing integration

**AI Copilot Development**
- White-label RAG solutions for client-specific use cases
- Leverage internal Private RAG Copilot IP
- Target industries: Legal, Healthcare, Financial Advisory

### 3. Operational Excellence

**Team Growth**
- Hire 8 senior AI engineers (2 per month through Q4)
- Onboard 4 business development representatives
- Target headcount: 65 by end of Q4 (currently 53)

**Process Optimization**
- Reduce client onboarding time from 6 weeks to 3 weeks
- Implement standardized project templates across all service lines
- Achieve 95% on-time project delivery (currently 87%)

**Knowledge Management**
- Deploy internal RAG system for consulting IP and best practices
- Digitize all client deliverables and case studies
- Create searchable knowledge base accessible to all consultants

## Risk Mitigation

### Market Risks
- **Risk:** Economic slowdown reducing client budgets
- **Mitigation:** Offer flexible payment terms, emphasize ROI-focused pilots

### Operational Risks
- **Risk:** Difficulty hiring qualified AI talent
- **Mitigation:** Expand remote hiring, offer competitive comp (top 25% of market)

### Competitive Risks
- **Risk:** Large consulting firms entering AI advisory market
- **Mitigation:** Focus on mid-market agility, deep technical expertise, faster time-to-value
